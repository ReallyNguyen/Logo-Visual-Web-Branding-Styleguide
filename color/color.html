Colour Palette Introduction

We have derived our colour scheme from a fusion of the hues present in our galaxy and the environment around us. By incorporating subtle variations of our primary colours, we aim to elevate the visual appeal of our app and provide an immersive user experience.

#462255 — Russian Violet

This will be our main representative colour portraying the vast expanse of space and the importance of protecting our planet. It also symbolizes the need to conserve water and reduce waste.

#C84C09 — Syracuse Orange

This colour represents the urgency of taking action on climate change. It also symbolizes the energy and innovation required to solve this global problem.

#FFD400 — Golden Yellow

This colour represents the sun and the importance of solar energy as a renewable resource. It also symbolizes hope and optimism for a sustainable future.

#D90368 — Dogwood Rose

This colour is used to compliment our primary colour and can be associated with the symbolism of love and care, which can provoke our users to interpret the meaning as caring for the environment.

#436436 — Hunter Green

This colour is used to compliment our primary colour and can be associated with the symbolism of love and care, which can provoke our users to interpret the meaning as caring for the environment.

#FFFFFF — Pure White

White is known to be the colour associated with cleanliness and purity. By making this part of our primary colour palette we can further emphasize the importance of eco-friendly habits; encouraging our users to keep their surroundings clean and free of pollutants.

Examples Presented in App

Our application will feature a dark-mode interface, where the majority of buttons will be displayed in white. Texts will seamlessly transition between our primary dark purple color and white, depending on the context of each page. Additionally, all other colors will blend together in a gradient, complemented by graphics showcasing the beauty of our galaxy on the landing page.

A few examples of our buttons and menu page has been placed above to show how our primary colours of purple and white will be utilized throughout our page. Graphics created in Illustrator will use the fusion of yellows, greens and orange further enhance the visual appeal of our page.
