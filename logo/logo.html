Logo Design Formation

Our logo features an astronaut holding a flag with an image of Earth as its main component. This logo represents the idea that we should strive to protect our environment not only on Earth but also beyond it.

Several concepts were sketched out with the objective of integrating our app's mascot into the logo. Inspired by Pixar’s logo with the lamp as it’s “L”. Considering the constraints on variations for the original concept, we have opted for our mascot holding a flag with an illustration of planet Earth for our logo. We have also included subtle details that further reinforces our goal of encouraging our users to adopt eco-friendly habits.
